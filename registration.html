🎁 Perks & Benefits

NSS Unit (₹500)

  • Exclusive participation in NGO Talks by national-level organizations (NAAM Foundation, Teach for India, Vanarai, 1M1B)
  • Opportunity to present annual tenure activities and achievements
  • Participation in Round Table Conference with NGO representatives and student leaders
  • Networking with multiple NSS units across Maharashtra
  • Certificates of participation and appreciation from SWDC, VIT Pune

Sociothon (₹500)

  • AICTE-approved virtual internship opportunities with 1M1B and associated NGOs
  • Certificates for all participants (including IBM SkillsBuild certificates)
  • National-level exposure & networking with NGOs, experts, and academicians
  • Recognition, mentorship, and learning opportunities through the PRISM platform
  • Prize Pool worth ₹20,000+ (₹10,000 – Winner | ₹6,000 – First Runner Up | ₹4,000 – Second Runner Up)

College Student (₹180)

  • Access to inspiring NGO Talks and Guest Sessions
  • Opportunity to attend the Panel Discussion featuring speakers like Prasad Gawade, Mayuresh Gujar, and Deepankar Patil
  • Participation in KalaKriti – cultural performances (music, dance, instrumental)
  • Hands-on experience through Ideathon – pitch innovative ideas
  • Exposure to social innovation, sustainability, and real-world problem-solving
  • Certificates of participation for all attendees

Event Registration Instructions

🔹 For VIT Students/Participants:

  1. Login at: using your PRN No. as login ID and password.
  2. Click on the “Event” tab.
  3. Select Organising Institute.
  4. Select Event and click on “Pay Fees”.
  5. Choose Payment Gateway “PAYTM” and proceed for payment.

🔹 For Other Students/Participants:

  1. Login at:
  2. Click on “Signup” and sign in using your Gmail account.
  3. Go to the “Pay Event Fees” tab.
  4. Choose the Organising Institute → Select VIT, Pune.
  5. Select your Event from the dropdown menu.
  6. Click on “Pay Fees” → Choose Payment Gateway “PAYTM” and proceed.

Note: Please verify all details before making payment.
